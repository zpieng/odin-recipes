Strawberry Fizz

Description

A zesty refreshment for afternoons at the height of summer.

Brings together fresh strawberries, local honey, and sparkling water

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups water
  • 1/8 cup sugar
  • 1/8 cup honey
  • 2 cups fresh strawberries
  • 1.5 cups sparkling water

Steps

  1. In a large pot, boil water.
  2. Stir in honey and sugar until dissolved.
  3. Turn off heat, add strawberries, and cover. Leave covered for at least 1 hour.
  4. Using the cover of the pot to keep the strawberries from falling out, pour the liquid into a bowl (or pitcher). At this point, you can discard or eat the strawberries themselves.
  5. Cover bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate.
  6. Once cooled, pour into glass and top with soda water for the fizz.
